Welcome, Young Explorers!!

Come join us at the Community Science Museum where we’re committed to making science accessible to all. 

Check out our activities ↓

Young Star

Science Club for the youngest!

Young Stars club which meets once a week on a Saturday between 10:00 and 13:00 where you’ll get to explore and experiment with our team of experts.  You can learn about Newtonian physics from our bumper swing, or why not travel back in time and meet our resident Woolly Mammoth? 

newtonian physics

Immerse yourself in the wonders of physics

You can learn about Newtonian physics from our bumper swing, or why not travel back in time and meet our resident Woolly Mammoth? Our exhibits are designed to be accessible for interested minds, so make sure you come ready to learn and explore. 

Discover the Universe

Learning science has never been so exciting!

Prepare for a cosmic exploration as you delve into the mysteries of the universe. Learn about distant planets, mesmerizing galaxies, and the wonders of space travel. Discover the awe-inspiring beauty and mind-bending phenomena that exist beyond our world.

Robotics and AI 

Science of the future

The information revolution is here and robotics and artificial intelligence are the science of the future. From useful home applications of AI to industrial uses of robotics, the future is here. You can even say hello and shake the hand of Rob the Robot.
